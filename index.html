About

We are a club from Rocklin High School wanting to introduce photography to beginners and intermediates while contributing to the community. We strive to be open and inclusive of everyone and give them opportunities to practice and showcase their skills while bringing something to the table. We hope that you will join us on this mission to use photography to tell stories and showcase the world.
